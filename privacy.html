Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Kakkodhamal, a fun and interactive Gujarati alphabet learning mobile application designed specifically for children. This Privacy Policy details how we treat information when you use our app.

🛡️ Privacy Commitment: We prioritize your child's privacy. While Kakkodhamal does not directly collect, store, or transmit personal information, the app partner-integrates with third-party advertising providers to support the application.

1. 100% Offline & Local Gameplay

Aside from fetching advertisements when an active internet connection is available, all learning progress, gameplay, and interactions remain stored 100% locally on your device.

2. No Account Registration

There are no sign-ups, registrations, or profiles required to use our application. Children can start learning instantly without entering any personal identifiers, emails, names, or phone numbers.

3. Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

To keep this app free, we display advertisements. We only partner with ad networks that support COPPA-compliant configurations for children's apps.

Non-Personalized Ads Only: We explicitly configure our ad providers to serve non-personalized or contextually relevant ads to ensure children's behavior is not tracked across websites or apps.

The ad networks may collect anonymous, technical device identifiers solely for ad frequency capping, contextual targeting, and fraud prevention.

4. Children's Privacy Compliance (COPPA & Google Play Families)

Our app is designed to fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policies.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

All ad traffic is flagged as Child-Directed at the SDK level to disable behavioral tracking.

5. Permissions

Depending on the ad network requirements, the app may utilize basic network access permissions (INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE) to fetch ads. It does not request access to sensitive features like your location, camera, microphone, or contacts.
