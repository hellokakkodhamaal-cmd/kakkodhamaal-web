🧱 About Us (અમારા વિશે)

કક્કો ધમાલ (Kakkodhamal) એ માત્ર એક ગેમ નથી, પણ બાળકોને તેમની માતૃભાષા સાથે જોડવાનો એક અનોખો પ્રયાસ છે. અમે ડિજિટલ સ્ક્રીન ટાઈમને અર્થપૂર્ણ લર્નિંગ ટાઈમમાં બદલવા માંગીએ છીએ.

🎯 અમારો હેતુ (Our Mission)

આજના ડિજિટલ યુગમાં બાળકો સરળતાથી, સુરક્ષિત રીતે અને રમતા રમતા શુદ્ધ ગુજરાતી ભાષા શીખી શકે તેવું વાતાવરણ પૂરું પાડવું.

🎨 યુનિક ક્લે-આર્ટ સ્ટાઈલ (Unique Clay-Art Style)

એપ્લિકેશનના દરેક અક્ષરો અને પાત્રોને વાસ્તવિક પ્લે-ડોહ (Playdough) જેવો અહેસાસ આપવા માટે ખાસ 3D ક્લે-આર્ટ શૈલીમાં તૈયાર કરવામાં આવ્યા છે, જે બાળકોની કલ્પનાશક્તિને ખીલવે છે.

🔒 સંપૂર્ણ સુરક્ષિત (100% Child-Safe)

કોઈ લોગ-ઈન નહીં, કોઈ ડેટા ટ્રેકિંગ નહીં અને કોઈ એડ્સ નહીં! બાળકો માટે તદ્દન સુરક્ષિત અને ઈન્ટરનેટ વગર (Offline) પણ ચાલે તેવી એક અનોખી શૈક્ષણિક એપ.

Privacy Policy for Kakko Dhamal

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Kakko Dhamal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially young children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application, Kakko Dhamal (the "App").

Because our App is designed specifically for children, we take extra precautions to protect their safety and privacy online. We strictly adhere to Google Play’s Families Policy and applicable child protection regulations.

1. Information We Do Not Collect (Children's Privacy)

Kakko Dhamal is designed strictly as a safe, family-friendly educational game for kids.

No Personal Data: We do not knowingly collect, store, or solicit any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise physical locations) from children.

No Personalized Ads Data: Because our app targets children, we explicitly disable the tracking of the Android Advertising Identifier (AAID) and do not pass any user-tracking data to ad networks.

No Account Creation: Children can play the entire app without creating an account or logging in.

2. Information Collected Automatically

To help us maintain the gaming experience, provide sound effects, and track progression milestones, the App processes minimal, non-personal technical data:

Local Game Progress: Your child's unlocked levels and simple words mastered are stored locally right on your Android device. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to an external server. No scores or high scores are tracked or stored anywhere.

Device Performance Information: We may use standard anonymized operational tools (such as Google Play Console analytics) to review crashes or bugs. This information contains no personal data and is only used to maintain technical stability.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To help keep this educational app free to use, we display limited, child-safe advertisements. We enforce strict safety measures to keep the workspace secure and distraction-free for children:

Strict "G" Content Rating: We only request contextual ads with a maximum content rating of G (General Audience). The ad content is filtered to ensure it is completely safe and appropriate for young children.

No Behavioral or Targeted Advertising: All personalized ads, interest-based tracking, and remarketing are entirely disabled. Ads are served purely based on the context of the app, not your child’s history.

Families Self-Certified Ad Networks: We only use Google Play Self-Certified Ads SDKs (such as Google AdMob configured for child-directed treatment). These providers may process non-personal data—such as ad impressions, clicks, and basic usage data—purely for operational purposes like ad frequency capping and fraud detection.

External Links: Any functional external links (such as a link to rate us on the Play Store) or ad-click destinations are secured behind an adult-gated parental control mechanism to prevent accidental navigation by children.

4. Data Retention and Security

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not store personal data on external cloud servers. All gameplay progression lives directly within the App storage on your physical device. If you delete or clear the App cache from your device settings, your local progress data will be permanently removed.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to align with new feature releases or regulatory changes. We encourage parents to periodically review this page for any updates.
